About me

I am a senior student at Brock University, St. Catharines, ON., working towards my BSc. (Honours) in Computer Science and Economics. My interests include all things tech, but I am not limited as my passions are widespread, also being an avid musician and coffee enthusiast.

Personal Projects

Relic

Relic is a mobile application that provides Brock University students a platform for buying and selling textbooks easily. It makes use of Android and iOS platforms, as well as a central MySQL database using PHP services for data transfer.

While originally for a capstone project course, myself and the associated developers have continued to pursue the idea to see how it may benefit local students. Read more here, or see below for a commercial outlining the application.

CS Breakdown

CS Breakdown is an educational series on topics in Computer Science ranging from advanced algorithm design to detailed mathematical proofs. The series aims to provide high quality, in depth, and understandable instruction on the selected topics to benefit future Computer Science students. Similar to Relic, this application also began as a collaboration for course work with an academic colleague, but has since grown beyond the scope of the classroom. It is hoped that in the near future we will have videos targeting people of all skill levels. Read more, or watch a video at the CS Breakdown Homepage.
